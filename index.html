CONGRATULATIONS !

SMILE LONGEVITY

noun
Smile longevity represents the ability to retain and maintain your natural teeth for your entire lifetime through the act of proper proactive care and smile maintenance. Our goal is to get you from today to age 100 by avoiding unexpected breakdown and tooth loss.

ORAL MICROBIOME

noun
The oral microbiome is home to twenty million bacteria and this community of microorganisms needs to work together. If they are imbalanced, this can have an adverse effect on immunity and overall health. Unruly bacteria, also known as biofilm, is a contributing factor to a long list of chronic inflammatory health issues not to mention affecting fertility and brain function. Being conscious about the health of your oral microbiome is half the battle in keeping your smile in tip-top shape.

BIOFILM

noun
A biofilm is a complex, communal, three-dimensional arrangement of bacteria that can be found and adheres to the surfaces of your teeth and can sneak below the gum line. It is a place where harmful bacteria hibernates. Our biofilm rating evaluates the areas that require professional attention. The enemy is biofilm and it is a sign of an unhealthy oral microbiome.

INFLAMATION TREATMENT

noun
inflammation treatment takes care of removing the bad bacteria and debris on the surface of your teeth and under your gums. We spend some time flushing out all contaminants and bring you back for a final shine. Your gums need love and chronic inflammation leads to a costly path of endless repair.

SURFACE BUILD UP REMOVAL

noun
Surface build-up removal is needed when it's been a while since your teeth and gums have been thoroughly and professionally cleaned. Severe inflammation is present, your gums are stressed and your oral microbiome is out of order. We will gently remove the build-up and move you into the next healing phase of therapeutic gum treatment.

THERAPEUTIC GUM TREATMENT

noun
Therapeutic gum treatment is a gentle deep clean below the gum line that includes laser rejuvenation to stamp out dangerous inflammation from causing further smile breakdown. Your gums provide vital support and also act as a protector of the enamel of each tooth. Over time and in the absence of professional hygiene visits, debris containing hard-to-reach bacteria can build up under the gums and wreak havoc causing severe inflammation and even loss of bone. Your gums are the gold standard of your smile.

GUM CONSULT

noun
Gum consults are needed when there is an issue with the integrity of your gums or bone that lie beyond our therapeutic treatments. Our periodontist is a specialist who analyzes and creates a recommended path as needed for better smile health and smile longevity.

ITERO SCAN

noun
Our iTero 3D scan is not only revolutionary but its high precision ability changes the way you see your smile care. You will be able to view for yourself, a complete real-time smile status with intricate details and the sum of all its parts. Visualization is a key component to your understanding of smile longevity and where you are today. Say goodbye to wondering what comes next.

CLIN-CHECK INVISALIGN

noun
The clin-check is a 3D animation movie to give you the information you need to visualize your new smile. It will show you your predicted results during and after your Invisalign treatment. The clin-check digitally transcribes your smile even before you wear your first Invisalign aligner.

CEREC CUSTOM TOOTH COVER

noun
Cerec custom tooth covers are made with 3D technology to create and install beautiful fitting crowns in just one visit. There's no gunky impression material to bite down on because the process is 100% digital and the results are amazing. You will end up with a crown that fits to a T with puzzle-piece precision.

VENEERS

noun
Veneers are great for both improving the aesthetics of your smile and protecting the surface of your teeth from damage. With our artistically crafted and expertly placed veneers, you can enjoy all of your everyday activities guilt-free because you've got a smile that will stand the test of time. And perhaps best of all, your confidence will skyrocket!

SLEEP APNEA

noun
Sleep apnea is a condition that occurs during sleep and stops normal breathing. It is typically caused by obstruction of the airway or a disturbance in the brain's respiratory center. When sleep is interrupted throughout the night, your immune system is challenged. In addition, breathing through your mouth is a side effect that can damage the surface of your teeth over time causing unwanted wear and tear of your enamel surfaces. Addressing sleep disorders such as apnea is an important factor in overall health as well as the health of your teeth.
